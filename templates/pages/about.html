About OurHome

OurHome is Kerala's first climate-smart construction platform. We help homeowners build right — choosing verified materials suited to their climate, finding trusted contractors, and verifying contractor quotes using AI.

Our Mission

Kerala loses thousands of crores every year to construction mistakes — wrong materials for the climate, overpriced quotes, unverified contractors. OurHome fixes all three in one platform.

What We Offer

  • Climate-smart materials catalog — 30+ verified building materials with climate suitability filters
  • Verified service providers — OurHome-verified contractors, architects, and interior designers
  • AI Bill Analyzer — upload your contractor's quote and catch overcharges instantly

OurHome is a functional prototype. Commercial launch planned for Q3 2026. For enquiries: hello@ourhome.in
