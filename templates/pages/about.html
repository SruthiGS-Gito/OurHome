About OurHome

Kerala's Construction Intelligence Platform

OurHome is Kerala's first climate-smart construction platform — built to bring transparency, verified expertise, and AI-powered tools to the state's fragmented construction industry. We help homeowners choose the right materials for their climate, discover verified contractors and architects, and catch overcharges in contractor quotes before a single brick is laid.

The Problem We Solve

Kerala's construction sector is worth thousands of crores — yet most homeowners build with almost no reliable information. Contractors quote inflated prices, materials get chosen based on brand familiarity rather than climate suitability, and there is no trustworthy way to discover or verify the professionals you hire.

  • OPC cement used in coastal zones corrodes faster, leading to cracked structures within a decade.
  • Contractors routinely overcharge on materials by 15–30% above market rates.
  • There is no centralised, verified directory of contractors, architects, and designers.

What OurHome Offers

  • Climate-Smart Materials Catalog

    30+ verified building materials with 5-zone climate suitability filters — coastal, hot & dry, heavy rainfall, cold & hilly, cyclone-prone. Prices sourced from Kerala PWD SOR 2024.

  • AI Bill Analyzer

    Upload your contractor's quote as a PDF or photo. Our AI (powered by Groq's llama-3.3-70b) reads every line item, checks it against Kerala market rates, flags overcharges, and generates a negotiation script.

  • Verified Service Provider Directory

    Browse OurHome-verified contractors, architects, and interior designers with portfolio photos, project history, specializations, and direct contact/inquiry forms.

  • Personalised Recommendations

    Based on your location, OurHome automatically surfaces the materials best suited to your climate zone — no manual filtering needed.

The Team

OurHome was designed and built by final-year B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering students at Sree Buddha College of Engineering, Pattoor (APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, KTU), graduating in 2026.

The project demonstrates a production-grade, AI-integrated full-stack web application using Django, MySQL, HTMX, Cloudinary, and the Groq AI API — built as an academic capstone project with real-world applicability.

OurHome is currently a functional academic prototype. Commercial launch planned for Q3 2026. For enquiries: hello@ourhome.in
