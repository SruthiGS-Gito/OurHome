About OurHome

Kerala's Construction Intelligence Platform

Our Mission

OurHome is a web-based platform designed to bring transparency and informed decision-making to Kerala's construction sector. We help homeowners research materials, verify professionals, and understand fair pricing before committing to any construction project.

What We Offer

  • Materials Catalog

    Browse verified building materials with climate suitability data for Kerala's five distinct climate zones. Prices are referenced from government-published rate schedules.

  • AI Bill Analyzer

    Upload a contractor quote and receive an instant analysis comparing line items against current market rates, with flagging for overpriced or unsuitable materials.

  • Professional Directory

    Discover OurHome-verified contractors, architects, and interior designers with portfolio work, specializations, and direct inquiry forms.

  • Smart Recommendations

    Get material suggestions tailored to your location and climate zone automatically.

The Team

OurHome is a capstone project developed by final-year B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering students at Sree Buddha College of Engineering, Pattoor, affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), graduating batch of 2026.

The platform is a fully functional prototype demonstrating AI-integrated full-stack web development with real-world applicability to the Kerala construction sector.
