Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 — OurHome v1.0 prototype

1. What Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and location (city, district, state) to provide personalised material recommendations and service provider matching.

If you are a service provider, we additionally collect your business name, years of experience, specializations, pricing range, and verification documents (government-issued ID, professional license, portfolio) to establish your verified listing.

If you use the AI Bill Analyzer, your uploaded contractor quotes (PDF or image) are temporarily stored on our server for the duration of your session and deleted automatically when you clear the analysis or close your session — always within 24 hours.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

Your account data and activity are stored in a MySQL database hosted on Railway's cloud infrastructure. Profile photos, portfolio images, and verification documents are stored on Cloudinary's CDN (cloud.cloudinary.com) — a GDPR-compliant media hosting service.

All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption). We do not store plaintext passwords — Django's password hashing (PBKDF2-SHA256) is used.

3. Who Can See What

  • Service provider profiles (name, business info, portfolio, specializations) are publicly visible to all logged-in users on the directory pages.
  • Inquiry contact details (name, email, phone, message) submitted via a provider's profile page are shared only with that specific provider — not with other users.
  • Verification documents (ID proof, license) are accessible only by the OurHome admin team for verification purposes.
  • Saved materials, browsing history, and reviews are private to your account.

4. Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. We use the following sub-processors to operate the platform:

  • Railway — cloud hosting and database
  • Cloudinary — media file storage and CDN
  • Groq — AI processing for the Bill Analyzer (your uploaded bill is sent to Groq's API; Groq's privacy policy applies)

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing privacy@ourhome.in. We will process deletion requests within 7 business days.

You may also request a copy of the data we hold about you at the same address.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@ourhome.in
